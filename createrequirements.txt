Now lets talk about phishing awareness.
 
When you get an email that says "Your bank account is blocked. Click this link to verify immediately" what do you do?
 
You need to be careful with these kinds of emails.
 
So is this email safe? You can answer yes or no.
 
If you say no that is correct. This is probably a phishing attempt.
 
If you say yes you need to be careful. This could be a phishing scam.
 
Here are some tips to stay safe from phishing:
 
* Check the senders email address
 
* Do not click on unknown links
 
* Never share your one-time password or passwords with anyone
 
Now lets check your password strength.
 
You can enter your password. We will check how strong it is.
 
We will give your password a score out of 4.
 
If your password is enough that is a point.
 
If it has numbers in it that is another point.
 
If it has letters that is a point too.
 
If it has special characters like !. @ That is another point.
 
Then we will tell you how strong your password is.
 
If you get a score of 4 that is a password.
 
If you get a score of 2 or 3 that is a password.
 
If you get a score of 0 or 1 that is a weak password.
 
Finally lets talk about some privacy tips.
 
* You should enable Two-Factor Authentication or 2FA, for short
 
* You should not use Wi-Fi for banking
 
* You should check what permissions your apps have regularly
 
* You should keep your software updated
 
* And you should not share your information online
 
These are some simple tips to help you stay safe online.

def phishing_awareness():
   print("\n=== Phishing Awareness ===")
   print("You receive an email saying:")
   print("'Your bank account is blocked. Click this link to verify immediately.'")
 
   answer = input("\nIs this safe? (yes/no): ").strip().lower()
 
   if answer == "no":
       print("✅ Correct! This is likely a phishing attempt.")
   else:
       print("⚠️ Warning! This could be a phishing scam.")
 
   print("\nTips to stay safe:")
   print("- Check sender email carefully")
   print("- Avoid clicking unknown links")
   print("- Never share OTP or passwords")
def password_checker():
   print("\n=== Password Strength Checker ===")
   password = input("Enter your password: ")
 
   score = 0
 
   if len(password) >= 8:
       score += 1
   if any(char.isdigit() for char in password):
       score += 1
   if any(char.isupper() for char in password):
       score += 1
   if any(char in "!@#$%^&*" for char in password):
       score += 1
 
   print("\nPassword Score:", score, "/ 4")
 
   if score == 4:
       print("✅ Strong Password")
   elif score >= 2:
       print("⚠️ Moderate Password")
   else:
       print("❌ Weak Password")
